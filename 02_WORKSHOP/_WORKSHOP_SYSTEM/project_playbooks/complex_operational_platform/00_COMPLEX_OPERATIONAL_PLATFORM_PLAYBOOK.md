# Complex Operational Platform Playbook

Use this playbook for large multi-actor operational projects. It is a lens only; the active project must specialize it.

Check for:
- actors and roles,
- lifecycle states,
- events,
- permissions,
- audit,
- workflows,
- data ownership,
- cross-module effects,
- realtime/notification needs,
- operational dashboards,
- reporting and governance,
- failure recovery.
