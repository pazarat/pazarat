# 360 Degree Project Method

The 360 method is the universal engineering method for all project types. It is not software-only.
Every important project must be viewed through its full lifecycle and impact surface.

## Core Axes

- intent and objective,
- beneficiaries and actors,
- source of truth,
- domain logic,
- workflows and scenarios,
- data or evidence model,
- roles, permissions, safety, or governance where relevant,
- implementation or delivery path,
- quality and acceptance,
- risks and failure modes,
- maintainability and future change,
- feedback and repair loop.

## Project-Specific Specialization

The 360 method does not impose one template on every project. It detects project type and then
activates suitable discipline canons and local standards generation.
